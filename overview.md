### 1. User Experience (Subscriber Dashboard)
* **Seamless Onboarding** – Secure Email/Password signup and login.
* **Live Statistics** – Real‑time IQ Option balance, active trades, and profit/loss visualizations.
* **Granular Bot Control** – Users can set trade amounts, toggle Martingale, switch between Practice/Real accounts, and start/stop the bot instantly.
* **Automated Billing** – Self-service portal to view plans, check next billing dates, and view payment history.
* **Performance Analytics** – Personal win-rate tracking and Martingale streak history.
* **Integrated Support** – Built-in form to report bugs or ask questions directly to the admin.

### 2. Management Suite (Admin Panel)
* **User Oversight** – Full control to list, edit, suspend, or delete users and generate access codes.
* **Revenue Tracking** – Financial dashboard to monitor Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) and export data.
* **System-Wide Analytics** – Monitor global win/loss rates and identify peak performance times.
* **Content Engine** – Built-in Blog management (Markdown) for SEO, news, and trading tips.
* **Health Monitoring** – Real-time system status, trade logs, and manual signal triggers for testing.

### 3. Payments & Security
* **Dual Gateways** – **Paystack** (for local NGN payments) and **Stripe** (for international USD and Crypto).
**Auto unsubscribe users** automatically unsubscripe users when their subscription expires 
* **Full Automation** – Webhook integration ensures that access is granted immediately upon payment and revoked automatically if a subscription expires.
* **Data Safety** – Industry-standard security; sensitive card details are never stored on our servers.

### 4. Branding, Marketing & SEO
* **Professional Presence** – Custom Homepage, Features, Pricing, and FAQ pages.
* **Personal Authority** – An "About" page highlighting your expertise to build trust with subscribers.
* **SEO Optimized** – Built with meta tags and sitemaps to help your platform rank on Google.
* **Transactional Email** – Automated, branded emails (Welcome, Invoices, Alerts) sent from your custom domain.

### 5. Infrastructure & Maintenance
* **Sync & Integration** – The web app will share the **MongoDB** database with the existing bot for a seamless experience.
* **Scalability** – As your user base grows, we will monitor database limits and upgrade hosting tiers as needed.
* **Reliability** – I will handle the ongoing maintenance of the web app alongside the bot to ensure 24/7 uptime.
* **Third-Party Costs** – external costs including Domain registration, Email hosting, and premium server/database fees.
